Prr Foundation

In a society where we are all running too fast to take a step back and look after our elderly, PRR foundation ensures that there is still some smiles left for our old people by providing them an environment which is devoted to take care of them, their needs and all their happiness.

A foundation that is aimed in serving elderly people with love and affection is now in a continuous process of expanding its branches to serve more old people who need help and aid those who are in need. We at PRR respect the fact that , with this vision of ours comes a great responsibility, And that's why we PRR’s are committed to serve with love and commitment that it brings joy into the lives of people no matter what their situation is.

Ensuring world class Healthcare facilities , 24 hour security and amenities that are required to have a peaceful old age, PRR Foundation comes with a warm and lively environment for people belonging to different reaches of society and people who have different traditions and cultures. With caring people all around every time, you can guarantee yourself an old-age which is bright, happy and carefree.

The main vision and mission of PRR Foundation is to ensure a peaceful old-age to everyone possible and provide with security and a worry free retirement, Moving Forward into a more beautiful Future!
