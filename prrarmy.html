Prr Army - #the_spark_to_ignite_smiles

“Joining hands , marching ahead, bringing help to the helpless, healing to the sick, guidance to the lost, meal to the hungry, water to the thirsty, that’s what PRR Army is all about – Moving forward spreading smiles!”

Welcome to PRR Army, where we the common people join together to bring a more positive impact in the society we live in. Impacting lives and bringing hope is what we are all excited about. Here in PRR army, we work to serve our community, beginning with a small spark we hope to ignite more lives and help our people.

We live in a world where one side there are so many who strive so hard for even a time’s meal, where there are so many who have no one to help them heal of their sickness, where there are people who are in need of a helping hand and on the other side we have people who really want to reach out, help, heal and feed those who need it the most, there are still some people who want to bring a smile on their faces. PRR Army acts as a bridge that connects these both sides, and bring together people who need help and people who are ready to help.

Humanitarian Services.

PRR Army involves itself in various humanitarian services to the common public. Reaching out even to the remotest of area’s (of Andhra’s geography), PRR Army has its wing of people serving the needful anywhere and everywhere. From infants to the oldest of people , PRR Army serves everyone belonging to various classes and communities of our society.

  • Natural calamities - During the times of natural calamities PRR army reaches out to every place that has been affected and helps the locals by providing the basic necessities such as food, clothing, shelter etc. An humble effort to serve those who have lost all their belongings to the natural disaster.

  • Medical camps - PRR Army reaches out to those who can not afford a proper health care by its various medical camps and free doctor check-up. With a mission that every single person gets the care he needs PRR Volunteers involve themselves in conducting various medical programs such as blood donation camp etc.

  • Educational services - A quality education for one child can bring out a positive effect on the society around him/her. This is why PRR Army strives to ensure that every child gets his/her chance for a fruitful education.

  • Awareness programs - PRR Army conducts various awareness programs and campaigns to ensure that everybody comes to know about the seasonal illness spreading, the ways to protect themselves, the rights they have, the duties they need to perform and about the opportunities they have in front of them. The main motive is to educate people about the various ways in which they can develop themselves and have a better living.

  • Women welfare - PRR Army engages in various programs to help women who need it, by forming various women help groups and patrols to meet the needs of women who couldn’t afford it. Things like free maternal care check-ups, basic education for grown up rural women are a part of PRR Army’s vision for women welfare.

    Come and join hands, let us all be a part of a movement that brings a positive impact in the lives of many and creating a better world for our future generation.
