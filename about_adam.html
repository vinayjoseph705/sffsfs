Rakesh Reddy

As the youth icon of Andhra Pradesh being a part of the idea of a Futuristic Andhra Pradesh, he believes that things work best when ordinary people have a voice, and when the government is answerable to the people who formed them. He has a vision for Andhra that works for many and not just for the privileged few. With a plan to make this vision into a reality P Rakesh Reddy moves forward to Move, together Andhra Pradesh Forward!.

Setting the standards in all his endeavours, and now for the Welfare of Andhra Pradesh. Working hard to move Andhra Forward, continuing his dedication towards towards the welfare of various sectors of Andhra and different minority communities alongside people under poverty line to move them forward. He looks forward to working with the people of Andhra as together they build a better Andhra. The challenges we face as a state are real. But there is no telling what we can achieve if we approach the fights ahead with passion and determination we feel today, and bringing that energy into 2018,2019,2020 and beyond. Forward! Promising Andhra ushering in a bright, new future now and for generations to come.
